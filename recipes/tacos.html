Pulled Pork Tacos

This is one of my favorite recipes and I will share with you how to make it

Ingredients

aka what you gonna need

  • Pork belly
  • Red cabbage
  • Creme Fraiche
  • Tacos
  • Jalapeno
  • Orange juice

    Preparation

    1. Preheat the oven for 220 degrees
    2. Put the pork belly in deep baking dish, put some orange juice over it
    3. Wait untill meat is tender and use two forks to pull it
    4. Prepare tacos on a pan and after they are toasted add creme fraiche and red cabbage
